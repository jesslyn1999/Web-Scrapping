Disinfectant Spraying Carried Out in Two Arterial Roads in Cilandak
Reporter : Mustaqim Amna | Editor : Maria Inggita | April 1st, 2020 12:40 PM 12:40 WIB | Dibaca 146 kali
(Foto : Mustaqim Amna / Beritajakarta.id)
South Jakarta Government sprayed two arterial roads in Cilandak Sub-district with disinfectant.
"  Jakarta now is in the state of emergency of COVID-19.
Thus we need to sterilize more massively"
As many as 25 South Jakarta Fire Handling and Rescue (Gulkarmat) Sub-Agency were deployed to sprayed disinfectant from Fatmawati traffic light to Ranco U-turn that leads to Jalan TB Simatupang.
"We also deployed one unit of high pressure pump truck with capacity 10,000 liters and medium-sized one with capacity 4,000 liters," stated Herbert Plider Lumbangaol, South Jakarta Gulkarmat Sub-Agency Head, Tuesday (3/31).
Disinfection activity was started from 7:30 AM
with assistance from civil apparatuses in urban villages, sub-district, Jakarta Transportation Agency (Dishub), South Jakarta Satpol PP, TNI and police.
"Jakarta now is in the state of emergency of COVID-19.
Thus we need to sterilize more massively," he asserted.
